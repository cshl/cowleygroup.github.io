Graduate students

Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory has a new bioAI PhD Program!
A Master's degree in a quantitative field is required.

Other opportunities for becoming a graduate student at CSHL and Stony Brook University:

If interested in any of these opportunities, please email cowley [at] cshl.edu, and include your CV and sample of your programming code.



Summer undergraduate students

Undergraduate students interested in joining CSHL for the summer may apply to the summer undergraduate research program (URP). The Cowley Lab usually takes one student per summer. Please email cowley [at] cshl.edu and include your CV.



High school students

Because our work requires advanced knowledge in mathematics and programming, we often do not consider high school students (and apologies if you do not hear back from us). CSHL does have a Partners for the Future Program where high school students can pursue research in CSHL labs.
